Our Story

Computer Solutions was founded in 2009 with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable, and affordable computer repair services to our community. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a trusted team of certified technicians.

We believe technology should work for you, not against you. That's why we focus on clear communication, transparent pricing, and quality repairs that last. Every device that comes through our doors receives the same care and attention to detail.

Over the years, we've expanded from basic repairs to comprehensive IT solutions, serving both residential customers and businesses of all sizes. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of over 10,000 customers.
